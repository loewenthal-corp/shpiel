Shpiel is deploying.

Point HF tooling at it:

  export HF_ENDPOINT=http://{{ include "shpiel.fullname" . }}.{{ .Release.Namespace }}.svc.cluster.local:{{ .Values.service.ports.api }}

Everything HF works unchanged from there:

  hf download org/model
  hf upload org/model ./local-dir

The chart's `config` value is Shpiel's config.yaml verbatim — see
config.example.yaml in the repository for the full reference. Remember to
keep `config.listen` in sync with `service.ports`, backend/xet paths in
sync with `persistence`, and to provide secrets via `env` (config only
ever references environment variable NAMES).
